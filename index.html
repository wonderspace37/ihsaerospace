Prometheus-Alpha

Prometheus-Alpha is an innovative CubeSat rideshare mission designed to provide cost-effective and flexible access to space for small satellite payloads. By leveraging shared launch opportunities, Prometheus-Alpha enables multiple CubeSat payloads to be deployed into orbit simultaneously, reducing individual mission costs and facilitating rapid deployment schedules. This collaborative approach not only maximizes the utilization of launch vehicles but also fosters a community of partners working together to advance space technology and research. Prometheus-Alpha's streamlined integration process and standardized interfaces ensure that partners can efficiently prepare their payloads for launch, making space more accessible than ever before.
