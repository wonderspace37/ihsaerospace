Join Our Team

We welcome students passionate about rocketry, engineering, and space exploration.

Membership Requirements

  • Strong interest in aerospace
  • Commitment to safety
  • Team player mindset
  • Willingness to learn

What We Offer

  • Hands-on engineering experience
  • Competition participation
  • Networking opportunities
  • Professional development

Support Our Mission

Your support helps us continue our mission of advancing aerospace education and innovation. Donations fund our competition entries, equipment, and educational outreach programs.

Donation Tiers

Booster - $50

  • Club newsletter

Orbital - $100

  • All Booster benefits
  • Team photo
  • Launch day invitation

Deep Space - $500+

  • All Orbital benefits
  • Name on our rocket
  • VIP launch viewing

Corporate Sponsorship

For corporate sponsorship opportunities, please contact us directly. We offer various partnership levels with benefits including:

  • Logo placement on rockets and team gear
  • Recognition at competitions
  • Recruitment opportunities
  • Team building workshops
